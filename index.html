So what is networking?

Formal Definition:

The exchange of information or services among individuals, groups, or institutions.

Better Definition:

The cultivation of productive relationships for employment and business.

Easy enough, right?

But what is a "productive" relationship?

Getting to the bottom of things...

Networking is

Effectively communicating with others in efforts to foster a mutually beneficial relationship with shared knowledge and resources.
