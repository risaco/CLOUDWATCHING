So what is networking?

Formal Definition:

The exchange of information or services among individuals, groups, or institutions.

Better Definition:

The cultivation of productive relationships for employment and business.

Easy enough, right?

But what is a "productive" relationship?

Getting to the bottom of things...

Networking is

Effectively communicating with others in efforts to foster a mutually beneficial relationship with shared knowledge and resources.

Who makes up the network?

Degrees of Networking

Ground Zero: Nuclear Network

HIGH VOUCH, MODERATE KNOWLEDGE

Those closest to you

  • Family Members
  • Close Friends
  • Close Co-Workers

1° : "Work" Circle

HIGH VOUCH, HIGH KNOWLEDGE

Those you have worked with in some capacity

  • Teammates
  • Co-Workers
  • Project Groups
  • Organizations

2° : I know a guy*

MODERATE VOUCH, MOD/HIGH KNOWLEDGE

Those you have met but haven't worked with

  • Conferences Attendees
  • Networking Events
  • Connected via mutual friend

3° : Peripheral Network

LOW VOUCH, MODERATE KNOWLEDGE

Those you have not met but may interact with

  • Social Media
  • Friends of 2° Friends
  • Members of Other Orgs
